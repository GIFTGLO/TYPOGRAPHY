A land breeze is a cool wind from the land blowing towards the adjacent water body at night. the land cools much faster than the water body at night. the warm moist air over the water body rises.cooler air from the land then blows towards the water body to fill the vacuum created by the rising air. this process creates a land breeze which cools the water body. a land breeze may lead to rainfall over water body and adjacent areas,usually night and early morning.
